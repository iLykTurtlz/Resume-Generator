For institutions who indicated that they were able to report another gender but the number students identified as another gender was less than 5, this variable/cell is blank and the corresponding item impuation flag variable (XEFYGUTO) would have the value of "S". 12-month Unduplicated Count by Gender Unknown or another gender are only reported for undergraduate and graduate levels and are not reported by full- or part-time status or the detailed degree/certificate-seeking undergraduate categories.

EFYGUKN - Total gender reported as one of the mutually exclusive binary categories (Men/Women)
Number of students for whom gender is known and falls into one of the mutually exclusive binary categories [Men/Women] enrolled for credit during the 12-month period. 12-month Unduplicated Count by Gender Unknown or another gender are only reported for undergraduate and graduate levels and are not reported by full- or part-time status or the detailed degree/certificate-seeking undergraduate categories. For institutions who indicated that they were able to report another gender but the number students identified as another gender was less than 5, this variable/cell is blank and the corresponding item impuation flag variable (XEFYGUKN) would have the value of "S".
